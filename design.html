glitch dream dungeon design document:

general design idealogue:

0. Various levels with different objectives: 
  a. (collect frogs, find a new friend by talking to them and making them like you, make a personalized web page, come to terms with your emotions ). 
    i. sword fight and platform challenge level
  b. Each completed objective rewards you with a artifact of that level (frog wizard staff, etc). 
  c. Artifacts are required to unlock certain warp gates (think super Mario 64). 
  d. Getting a minimum (not all) amount to unlock final boss

1. First level: collect hidden frogs in a Pokemon snap puzzle forest river area. 
  a. Each frog a unique color/look and musical tone. 
  b. Npc safari bug catchers scattered around. 
    i. Npc all look different and have personalities and reflect upon you player character.  

2. Warp gates scattered throughout level let you instantly transport to a new level 
  a. (gate remains, but competition of level objective isn't necessary.)
  b. Warp gates allow levels to intertwine. 
  c. Find eventually a travel arcane book that lets you teleport to previously found gate areas from your memory.

3. All throughout you have a magic tome that can only carry one spell at a time. 
  a. Find upgrades to allow it to hold more spells. Earlier challenges can be hacked with spells located in other levels

4. During development, mercilessly steal graphics and music and gameplay ideas as you compose and remix a weird interconnected dream vaporwave mesh of experiences, then during refinement and testing, make non copyright versions of them
